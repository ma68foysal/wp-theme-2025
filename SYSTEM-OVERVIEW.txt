PROJECT SUMMARY:
================================================================================

A production-ready video streaming platform featuring:
- Netflix-inspired dark theme with responsive design
- Complete member dashboard with watch history & preferences
- Advanced search & filtering capabilities
- Video progress tracking & continue watching
- Membership management with Stripe integration
- Video delivery via Bunny.net CDN with HLS streaming
- RESTful API for mobile app integration
- Professional administration interface

Total Codebase: ~15,000 lines
CSS: 1,200+ lines (fully responsive)
JavaScript: 550+ lines (optimized)
PHP Backend: 600+ lines custom functionality
Database Tables: 3 custom tables (memberships, payments, trials)

USER FLOWS:
================================================================================

HOMEPAGE VISITOR:
1. Lands on hero section
2. Sees featured video
3. Searches or browses by category
4. Views video grid
5. Clicks video to play
6. Sees paywall (login required)
7. Redirected to login
8. Creates account or logs in

LOGGED-IN MEMBER (NO SUBSCRIPTION):
1. Homepage shows paywall
2. Navigates to subscribe page
3. Selects plan (Standard or Premium)
4. Pays via Stripe
5. Membership activated
6. Redirected back to video
7. Can now watch content

PREMIUM MEMBER:
1. Watches videos freely
2. Progress auto-tracked
3. Can add to favorites
4. View history recorded
5. Recommended videos shown
6. Access to dashboard
7. Can manage subscription

MEMBER DASHBOARD ACCESS:
1. Click profile menu in header
2. Opens dropdown
3. Clicks "My Profile" or "Watch History"
4. Goes to /dashboard
5. Sees 4 tabs:
   - Watch History (all viewed videos)
   - Continue Watching (in progress)
   - My Favorites (saved videos)
   - Settings (account & preferences)
